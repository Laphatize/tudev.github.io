TUDev presents:
Code@Night

A bi-weekly event on Wednesdays nights when Temple's hacker community gathers to create, learn, and have fun.

Who: TUDev and others at Temple who like making things or learning about technology.
Where: Room 358 (the student lounge) at Science Education and Research Center (SERC)
(1925 N. 12th Street, Philadelphia, PA 19122)
When: Bi-weekly (every other week) on Wednesday nights from 5pm-9pm.
Cost: FREE!
What should I bring?
Hackers should bring their own devices (laptops, phones, any hardware they may need, chargers, etc.), and anything they want to work on.
Questions? Contact tudev@temple.edu

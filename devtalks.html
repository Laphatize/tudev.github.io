TUDev presents:
Devtalks & Workshops

Devtalks are held during most Code@Nights, and are short presentations on a technology, concept, company, project, etc. given by anyone interested in talking about a topic they're passionate about!

Workshops are longer sessions with fewer participants, and more of a typical lab or classroom. The workshop is more interactive, and the presenter takes questions throughout and helps students work through a problem or create a project live with the class. There are also usually assistants walking around helping out students that need it.
